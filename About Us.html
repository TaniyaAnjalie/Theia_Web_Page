About Us

"Jewelry has the power to be this one little thing that can make you feel unique"



Being jewellers is an amazing privilege for us. We play a role in many significant milestones in peoples lives.

All of our jewellery is designed and make in our workshop in Sri Lanka. Never taking the easy route.drawing the jewellers in the workshop crazy with the amount of components and numerous moving parts in each piece. However it is these intricate details that gives the jewellery it's unique style and why our customers come back to us time and time again.


From the begining, our mission has been fine jewelry for every day, for our damn selves.


We believe in jewelry as an expression of self: that it can mean whatever you want; that you can wear it when you want, buy it when you want, gift it, celebrate with it, reward yourself with it and keep it forever.


Why handmade?

When shopping hand-made, the piece you purchase is filled with the energy of hard work, hours of inspiration and a part of someone’s soul. Connection is everything and we are so grateful to be able to share the energy of the human spirit, and the sparkle of nature, with you
